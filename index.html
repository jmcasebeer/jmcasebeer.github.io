Jonah Casebeer

Hi there, I’m a second year PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC). I also completed my undergraduate studies at UIUC where I majored in Statistics & Computer Science. I’m particularly interested in signal processing and machine learning and especially enjoy their applications in audio problems.

I am advised by Paris Smaragdis and have done some work with Ivan Dokmanic. Feel free to check out what we’ve done on my publications page or my Google Scholar page.
